🪨 Part 1 · What are Rocks?

What are Rocks?

🪨⛰️

The Earth is made up of hard things called rocks. Rocks are found on land, in rivers and in seas. They come in many shapes, sizes and colours!

✨ Big Word: ROCKA hard, natural material that the Earth is made of. Rocks are made up of minerals.

🏆 Part 4 · Hardest & Softest

The Hardest & the Softest

🧈TalcSOFTEST
soft → → → hard
💎DiamondHARDEST
💎Diamond is the HARDEST mineral of all. It is so hard it can even cut glass! It is shiny and precious, so we make jewellery from it.
🧈Talc is the SOFTEST mineral. It is so soft it can be crushed into a fine powder!

✏️ The rock that rubs off!

Chalk is so soft that it wears off easily when you rub it — that's how it writes on the board and how teachers' hands get dusty!

🧠Remember: Pumice floats on water 🪨💧 and Chalk wears off when rubbed ✏️. These show up in Olympiad questions!
🕌 Part 7 · Rocks Around Us

Rocks Build Beautiful Things

🕌The famous Taj Mahal is made of beautiful white marble — a hard rock!
🗽Many statues, temples and buildings are made from strong rocks like marble and granite.
🏔️Rocks like gneiss, granite and sandstone make up our mountains.

🌟 Super Memory Tricks

🪨 The Earth is made of rocks; rocks are made of minerals.
🧱 Hard rocks: granite, sandstone, marble. Soft rocks: coal, chalk.
💎 Diamond = HARDEST mineral. Talc = SOFTEST mineral.
🛁 Pumice floats on water. ✏️ Chalk rubs off easily.
💍 Diamond → jewellery, ✏️ graphite → pencil, 🫙 silica → glass.
